About Kenya JUG

Building a vibrant Java community across Kenya, connecting developers, sharing knowledge, and fostering innovation.

Our Mission

To create a thriving ecosystem for Java developers in Kenya through weekly virtual sessions, LeetCode Wednesdays, JEP Thursdays, speaker opportunities, and collaborative open source projects.

Our Vision

To be the leading Java community in East Africa, driving innovation and excellence in software development across the region.

Our Values

Collaboration, continuous learning, inclusivity, and knowledge sharing. We believe in empowering every developer to reach their full potential.
